Bolognese Spaghetti

Home

Description

Enjoy this classic bolognese recipe that everyone and their mother knows how to do.

But stay put, as the secret to the italian version of this dish is revealed for its world-famous flavor

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (the white kind not the cheap yellow-y kind)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef from grass-fed cows
  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices
  • A square of black chocolate

Steps

  1. Pour the pasta into medium heat water and let it rest while we do the rest
  2. Grab a frying pan and insert your pre-marinated ground beef
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Grab the now al-dente spaghetti and pour it in the frying pan
  5. Add a bit of milk and black chocolate to cut the acidity
  6. Add some mozzarella on top of the completed dish and some herbs of your choosing

Enjoy this staple of italian cuisine
