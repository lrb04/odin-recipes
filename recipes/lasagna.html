Description

Your classic italian lasagna made fresh with home grown ingredients. Newly planted pestos garnish the dish, giving it a tangy flavour.

My grandma told me this recipe was passed down for generations, and the world should know it!

Ingredients

  • Provolone Cheese
  • Homegrown Cherry Tomatoes
  • Ground beef(cow or pork)
  • Rosemary
  • Black Olives

Steps

  1. Make a bed of cheese that covers the whole bottom
  2. Add ground beef so it covers the whole bed
  3. Progressively add layers of cheese and meat until you run out of meat or cheese
  4. Make tomato sauce BY HAND until its a gooey paste, and keep it on low heat for a while
  5. Pre-heat the oven to 150Âª and insert the casserolle
  6. After two hours take it out, and pour the steamy tomato sauce on top
  7. Embellish the dish with rosemary and olives on top

Enjoy your classic Italian dish!
